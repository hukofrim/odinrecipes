Pasta

Description

This recipe will guide you to make pasta within 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Any type of pasta you prefer
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Canned tuna
  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as instructed on the packaging.
  2. Heat up pan.
  3. Add olive oil.
  4. Drain the canned tuna and saute with basil and thyme. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add cream to tuna. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the creamy tuna.
  7. Serve.
