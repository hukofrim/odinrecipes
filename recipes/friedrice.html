Fried Rice

Description

This recipe will guide you to make fried rice without having to go to the grocery store.

Needs cold leftover rice. No need to ruin perfectly fresh rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice from the refrigerator.
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil or butter
  • Any meat you have in your fridge. (Hotdog, chicken, ham, beef)
  • literally any spice or herb or vegatable you have available

Steps

  1. Heat up pan.
  2. Add oil.
  3. If you have garlic or onion, saute it. Otherwise, skip to the meats
  4. Add vegetables if you have them. Saute thoroughly.
  5. Add the leftover rice. Break down rice blocks into grains
  6. Add salt, peppers, other spices, and any herbs.
  7. Mix thoroughly and serve with a spoon.
