Macaroni Salad

Description

This recipe will guide you to make a simple, yet delicious macaroni salad.

Tastes better after being stored in the fridge overnight

Ingredients

  • Salad Macaroni (Other similarly-shaped pasta will work, too).
  • Salt
  • Red Onion
  • Mayonnaise
  • Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Pineapple
  • Raisins
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni as instructed on the packaging. Set aside.
  2. Boil the chicken and shred.
  3. Grate the carrots and cheese.
  4. Mince the red onion into fine pieces. Or use a food processor.
  5. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients together.
  6. Mix gently but thoroughly. Refrigerate overnight.
